How does SVC work?

On the market today, there are few calculators that can estimate the value of shares, and it is therefore difficult and time-consuming for individuals to familiarize themselves with shares and the stock market. This website is designed to, based on calculations and data that is either available on the internet or subjective estimates, calculate the value of an individual share and thus facilitate in deciding whether a share has a reasonable price or not.
